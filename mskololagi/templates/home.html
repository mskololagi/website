About Us

Founded in 1969, M S Kololagi began its journey as a humble coconut distribution company, driven by a passion for quality and customer service. Over the years, we have expanded our horizons, diversifying into various sectors while maintaining our core values. Initially specializing in coconut distribution, we ventured into agarbatti distribution, establishing strong partnerships with brands like Cycle Agarbatti and Bigg Agarbatti, which helped us solidify our position in the market.

Our growth trajectory continued with the addition of Samsung mobile distribution to our portfolio, enabling us to cater to a wider audience. We further broadened our reach with Sunpure oil distribution, and we proudly ventured into the rangoli, kumkum, and camphor businesses, tapping into cultural markets and celebrating traditions.

Today, we stand as a trusted partner across multiple industries, committed to delivering excellence, innovation, and sustainable practices. Looking ahead, we aim to continue expanding our product offerings and enhancing customer experiences, ensuring that we remain at the forefront of the industries we serve. Join us on our journey as we build a legacy of trust and quality, serving communities and contributing to their growth.

Welcome to our wholesale shop! Established in 1969, we specialize in Samsung distribution, Cycle Agarbatti distribution, Bigg Agarbatti distribution, Camphor distribution, use-and-throw items distribution, and Sunpure oil distribution. We are proud coconut merchants.
